# Kinetic Gain Embedded

Board Decision Accountability Ledger is designed to open an embedded executive-intelligence engagement:

- convert handoff sprawl, owner drift, and override activity into explicit board-visible accountability tradeoffs
- expose where named owner-of-record discipline can recover decision speed before another lane is funded
- tie ledger coverage and board confidence to named operating surfaces instead of abstract accountability complaints
- convert scattered accountability bottlenecks into one reusable intervention packet for boards and investors
